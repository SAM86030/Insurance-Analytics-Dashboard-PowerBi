# 🛡️ Insurance Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

 
## 📊 Project Overview
 
This project presents an interactive Insurance Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI. The dashboard analyzes policy performance, claim trends, customer activity, and overall business efficiency across multiple report pages.
 
The project is designed to simulate a real-world insurance company environment and demonstrates how data visualization helps in understanding claims, premiums, and customer behavior.
  
##  Objectives
 
- Analyze total premium collected from policies  
- Monitor total claim amount and claim frequency  
- Track active vs inactive policies  
- Compare performance across different policy types  
- Track claim status such as Pending, Rejected, and Settled  
- Analyze customer demographics (age and gender)  
- Provide interactive insights using filters and slicers  

  
##  Features & Highlights
 
### 1️ Business Problem
 
Insurance companies handle large volumes of policies and claims daily. Without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to track claim performance, customer distribution, and premium trends. Businesses need clear dashboards to understand operations and improve decision-making.
 
### 2️ Goal of the Dashboard
 
The goal of this dashboard is to analyze insurance data by visualizing premiums, claims, customer details, and policy performance to gain meaningful business insights.
 
### 3️ Walkthrough of Key Visuals
 
### 📍 Page 1: Overview Dashboard
 
- KPI Cards show important metrics such as Total Premium Amount, Total Coverage Amount, and Total Claim Amount  
- Policy Type Analysis compares premium across Travel, Health, Auto, and Life policies  
- Active vs Inactive Policies visualizes policy distribution  
- Claim Status Analysis shows Pending, Rejected, and Settled claims  
- Age Group Analysis highlights claim amount by customer age group  

### 📍 Page 2: Customer & Policy Details
 
- Detailed Data Table provides customer-level information including policy, premium, and claim details  
- Enables deeper analysis using filters like Customer ID, Policy Number, and Claim Number  

### 📍 Page 3: Feedback Analysis
 
- Word Cloud highlights frequently used customer feedback words  
- Rating Chart shows overall customer satisfaction levels  

  
### 4️ Business Impact
 
This dashboard helps businesses:
 
- Identify high-performing policy types  
- Monitor claim trends and settlement rates  
- Understand customer demographics  
- Improve operational efficiency  
- Support data-driven decision-making  
 

 
### 5️ Insights
 
Some insights that can be derived from the dashboard include:
 
- Certain policy types generate higher premium revenue  
- A portion of claims remains pending, affecting efficiency  
- Customer distribution varies across age groups  
- Feedback data highlights strengths and improvement areas  
 

  
##  Key Metrics
 
- Total Premium Amount  
- Total Coverage Amount  
- Total Claim Amount  
- Number of Policies  
- Active vs Inactive Policies  
- Claim Status Distribution  
 

  
##  Interactive Features
 
- Filters for Policy Number, Claim Number, and Customer ID  
- Dynamic filtering across all visuals  
- Easy navigation across multiple report pages
